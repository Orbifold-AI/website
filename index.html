What we do

Data Distillation

Enterprises have accumulated vast amounts of data - sometimes in petabytes. This creates a significant gap for LLM readiness. We make efficient data via distillation.

Data Augmentation

Enterprises need more data, often missing critical points, much like salt and vitamins in food. We identify the missing data points and supplement them to make the dataset diversified and healthier.

Multimodality

Rich data is often in multimodality. Preparing the data in multimodal fashion makes models contextual and powerful.
